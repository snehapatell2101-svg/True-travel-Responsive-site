Destination with Image

CANADA

Canada's features include its vast size,
longest coastline in the world, and a diverse landscape with mountains, plains, and forests.

It is the second-largest country globally, bordered by three oceans
(Pacific, Atlantic, and Arctic), and contains a significant portion of the world's freshwater.

DUBAI

The city has a large real estate market, especially in the luxury segment. Dubai's economy centres on trade, tourism, aviation, financial services, and real estate. The Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) is one of the world's major financial centres. In 2024, Dubai was the seventh most-visited city globally.

AFRICA

"Afrika" is the historical spelling of Africa, the second-largest
and second-most populous continent in the world. It is a
diverse continent with 54 countries, rich in culture, and has
a complex history, including the Afrika Korps and modern
organizations like Afrika Tikkun and ForAfrika. It can
also refer to a 2022 film or a video game.

RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan notes include its history as Rajputana, its formation on March 30, 1949, its location in northwestern India bordering Pakistan and several other states, and its geography dominated by the Aravalli Range and the Thar Desert. Key cultural aspects include the capital city of Jaipur, its famous attractions like Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort, and its rich traditions of folk music and dance.

MALDIV

The Maldives is an independent island nation in the north-central
Indian Ocean, known for its coral atolls and tourism-based
economy. The official language is Dhivehi, but English is widely
spoken and used in commerce and government schools.

JAIPUR

Jaipur is the capital of the Indian state of Rajasthan, known as the "Pink City," and is famous for its historic forts, palaces, and vibrant markets. It was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Jai Singh II and was one of India's earliest planned cities, featuring a grid layout based on Vedic architecture.
